# The Purpose of Resource Monitors in Snowflake

**Description:**
Resource monitors in Snowflake allow you to track and manage compute resource usage, helping you optimize costs and performance. They provide insights into warehouse usage, enabling you to make informed decisions about resource allocation. By setting thresholds and notifications, you can proactively manage resource utilization.

### Typical Scenario
A company wants to monitor and optimize the usage of its Snowflake warehouses to reduce costs. They create a resource monitor to track compute resource usage and set thresholds to notify them when usage exceeds a certain limit.
