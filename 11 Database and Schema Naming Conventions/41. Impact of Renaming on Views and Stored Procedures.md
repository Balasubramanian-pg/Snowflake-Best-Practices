# Impact of Renaming on Views and Stored Procedures in Snowflake

**Description:**
This topic discusses the effects of renaming database objects on views and stored procedures in Snowflake. When a table or schema is renamed, dependent objects like views and stored procedures may become invalid or require updates. This document provides guidance on handling such renames and minimizing disruptions.

## Short Explanation
**Overview:** Renaming database objects in Snowflake can impact dependent views and stored procedures.

**Problem Solved:** Understanding how renames affect dependent objects helps prevent errors and ensures data continuity.

**Importance:** Proper handling of renames is crucial for maintaining data integrity and minimizing downtime in analytics and warehousing workflows.

**Use Cases:**
- Renaming a table used in a view
- Renaming a schema containing stored procedures

### Typical Scenario
A business needs to rebrand its product line, requiring a rename of the underlying tables. However, views and stored procedures referencing these tables must be updated to reflect the change without disrupting ongoing analytics workflows.
