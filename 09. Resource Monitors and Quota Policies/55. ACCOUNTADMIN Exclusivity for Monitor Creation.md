# ACCOUNTADMIN Exclusivity for Resource Monitor Creation in Snowflake

**Description:**
In Snowflake, the ACCOUNTADMIN role has exclusive privileges to create and manage resource monitors. This ensures that only authorized administrators can configure and monitor resource usage, preventing uncontrolled costs and optimizing warehouse performance.

A Snowflake administrator wants to create a resource monitor to track daily warehouse costs and alert when costs exceed a certain threshold. The administrator, with the ACCOUNTADMIN role, creates the resource monitor and configures the alert.
