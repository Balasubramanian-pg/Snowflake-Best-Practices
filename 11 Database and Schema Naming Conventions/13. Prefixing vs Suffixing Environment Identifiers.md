# Prefixing vs Suffixing Environment Identifiers in Snowflake

**Description:**
This topic discusses the best practices for including environment identifiers in Snowflake object names, specifically whether to prefix or suffix them. Environment identifiers help distinguish between development, testing, and production environments. The choice between prefixing and suffixing environment identifiers impacts object naming conventions and can affect Snowflake object management.

## Short Explanation
**Overview:** The decision to prefix or suffix environment identifiers in Snowflake object names affects organization and management of objects across different environments.

**Problem Solved:** Ambiguity between objects in different environments, such as development, testing, and production.

**Importance:** Proper identification of environment-specific objects ensures data integrity and reduces errors in Snowflake object management.

**Use Cases:**
- Development and production environment separation
- Disaster recovery and data replication across environments

### Typical Scenario
A company uses Snowflake for data warehousing and has separate environments for development, testing, and production. To avoid confusion and ensure data integrity, they need to implement a consistent naming convention that includes environment identifiers.

In this example, 'DEV_' is a prefix indicating that 'MyDatabase' is in the development environment. Alternatively, 'MyDatabase_PROD' would use a suffix to indicate the production environment.
