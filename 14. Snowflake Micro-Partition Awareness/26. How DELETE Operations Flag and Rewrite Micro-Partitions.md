# How DELETE Operations Flag and Rewrite Micro-Partitions in Snowflake

**Description:**
This topic explains how DELETE operations in Snowflake affect micro-partitions, including how they are flagged and rewritten. When a DELETE operation is performed, Snowflake does not immediately remove the deleted data from micro-partitions. Instead, it flags the micro-partitions for rewriting. The micro-partitions are then rewritten to exclude the deleted data, optimizing storage and query performance.
