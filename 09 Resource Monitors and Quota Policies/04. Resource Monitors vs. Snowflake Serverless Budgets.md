# Resource Monitors vs. Snowflake Serverless Budgets

**Description:**
This topic compares and contrasts Resource Monitors and Serverless Budgets in Snowflake, two features that help manage costs and resources. Resource Monitors track and alert on credit usage, while Serverless Budgets provide a more granular way to manage costs for specific warehouses and roles. Understanding the differences between these features is crucial for effective cost management in Snowflake.
