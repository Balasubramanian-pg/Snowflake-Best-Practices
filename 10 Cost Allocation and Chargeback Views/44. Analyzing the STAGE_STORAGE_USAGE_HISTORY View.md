To analyze storage usage for a specific stage over the past month, you can use the following query:
```sql
SELECT STAGE_NAME, STORAGE_USAGE, USAGE_DATE
FROM STAGE_STORAGE_USAGE_HISTORY
WHERE STAGE_NAME = 'my_stage'
AND USAGE_DATE >= DATE_TRUNC('month', CURRENT_DATE - INTERVAL '1 month');
```

- The query selects the stage name, storage usage, and usage date from the STAGE_STORAGE_USAGE_HISTORY view.
- It filters the results to only include data for the specified stage and for the past month.
