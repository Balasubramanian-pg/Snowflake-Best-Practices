# Understanding Overlaps Metric in Clustering Health

**Description:**
The Overlaps metric in Snowflake measures the degree of overlap between micro-partitions in a table, helping to evaluate the effectiveness of clustering. A lower Overlaps value indicates better clustering, while a higher value suggests poor clustering. Understanding Overlaps helps optimize storage and query performance. By analyzing Overlaps, you can identify opportunities to improve clustering and reduce query execution times.

## Short Explanation
**Overview:** The Overlaps metric assesses clustering quality by measuring micro-partition overlap.

**Problem Solved:** Poor clustering can lead to slower queries and inefficient storage; Overlaps helps identify these issues.

**Importance:** Optimizing clustering improves query performance, reduces costs, and enhances data management.

**Use Cases:**
- Optimizing data warehouse storage

- Evaluating clustering effectiveness

A data analyst wants to evaluate the clustering health of a large sales fact table to optimize query performance. They use the Overlaps metric to identify areas for improvement and re-cluster the table accordingly.
