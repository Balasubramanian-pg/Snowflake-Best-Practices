# Naming Conventions for Zero-Copy Clones in Snowflake

**Description:**
This topic discusses best practices for naming zero-copy clones in Snowflake, ensuring clear identification and management of cloned databases, schemas, and tables. A well-structured naming convention helps in distinguishing between original and cloned objects, facilitating efficient data management and reducing confusion. In Snowflake, zero-copy clones are exact duplicates of existing objects, and a proper naming convention is essential for maintaining organization and data integrity. By following these guidelines, users can effectively manage their cloned environments.
