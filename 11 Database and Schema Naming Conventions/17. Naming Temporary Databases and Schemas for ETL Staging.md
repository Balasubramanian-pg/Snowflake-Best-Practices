# Naming Temporary Databases and Schemas for ETL Staging in Snowflake

**Description:**
This topic explains the best practices for naming temporary databases and schemas used for ETL (Extract, Transform, Load) staging in Snowflake. Proper naming conventions help in organizing and managing ETL workflows efficiently. Temporary databases and schemas are used to stage data during the ETL process before loading it into the final target systems.
